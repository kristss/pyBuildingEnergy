EN 14511 / EN 14825 Product Performance Implementation Audit

This page documents the product-performance layer used by the heat-pump examples. It is designed so that the user can inspect the standard, the code and the output files together.

Scope note: the implementation normalizes EN 14511 rating points and applies the EN 14825 part-load COP/EER correction used by the building-system simulation. It does not yet calculate the complete regulatory SCOP/SEER labelling procedure including thermostat-off, standby, crankcase-heater and off-mode hours.

Standards Used

StandardUsed ForImplementation Location
BS EN 14511-1:2022 Terms for capacity, effective power input, COP, EER, rated capacity and rating conditions. HeatPumpPerformanceDataCalculator._normalize_points()
BS EN 14511-2:2022 Air-to-water rating conditions. The examples use the heating tables for water outlet temperature levels and the cooling table for chilled-water operation. heat_pump_performance_data() in examples/heat_pump_15316_4_2_example.py
BS EN 14825:2022 Part-load rating points, capacity ratio and COPbin/EERbin degradation correction for water-based units. en14825_part_load_factor(), HeatPumpSystemCalculator, CoolingGenerationSystemCalculator

EN 14825:2022, SEERon and SCOPon bin weighting, Formulas (6), (16), (17) The same bin-specific performance concept is used for simulation electricity: E = Q / COPbin or E = Q / EERbin. HeatPumpSystemCalculator, CoolingGenerationSystemCalculator

Calculation Flow

  1. The example creates EN 14511/EN 14825-style heating and cooling rating rows for the selected scenario.
  2. HeatPumpPerformanceDataCalculator normalizes aliases, calculates missing COP/EER or input power and exports declared capacity/COP/EER maps.
  3. The same rating rows are augmented with capacity_ratio, part_load_factor and performance_at_part_load for audit plots.
  4. The heating/DHW heat-pump calculator interpolates declared COP and capacity from the map at each EN 15316-4-2 temperature bin.
  5. If part_load_performance_method="en14825", runtime capacity ratio is calculated from supplied heat divided by available capacity-hours, and COP is corrected to COPbin.
  6. The EN 16798-13 cooling-generation calculator performs the analogous EER correction for mechanical cooling operation.

Audit Checklist

  • Check EN 14511-2:2022 Tables 12 to 16 against the temperatures in heat_pump_performance_data().
  • Check EN 14825:2022 Formula (10) and Formula (23) against en14825_part_load_factor().
  • Check that the default air-to-water degradation coefficient is 0.9 unless overridden by measured data.
  • Check performance_14511_14825_rating_points.csv and visuals/00_performance_14511_14825.html before inspecting downstream generation results.
